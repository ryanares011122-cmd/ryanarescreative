How I Work

制作流程

How I Work

这不是单张图或单条视频的展示。更重要的是把需求拆成能生成、能返修、能归档、能交付的步骤。

This is not just about one image or one video. The focus is turning requirements into steps that can be generated, refined, archived, and delivered.

BriefPromptQCVersionDelivery
1
需求与画面拆解Brief Breakdown先确认剧情、角色、场景、道具和交付比例。Clarify story, characters, scenes, props, and output formats.
2
素材生成与版本整理Asset Generation通过提示词、参考图和返修记录控制素材可用性。Use prompts, references, and iteration notes to keep assets usable.
3
镜头返修与质量检查Shot Refinement关注脸、动作、站位、光影、台词归属和正反打连续性。Check faces, actions, positions, lighting, dialogue alignment, and continuity.
4
归档与后续复用Archive & Reuse把资产、视频、海报和项目说明整理成后续可复用的档案。Archive assets, videos, posters, and notes for future reuse.
